Risotto Recipe

Description

This risotto will blow yopur mind

Ingredients

  • Arborio Rice
  • White Wine
  • Thyme
  • Mushrooms
  • Chicken Stock
  • Parmigianno Regiano
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Warm olive oil in a cast iron skillet
  2. Saute rice until lightly toasted
  3. add garlic and thyme, cook until fragrant
  4. Season lightly as appropriate
  5. Deglaze with a mellow white wine
  6. Add stock one cup at a time and stir continously
  7. Add mushroom
  8. When all stock is absorbed stir in butter, season and add cheese
